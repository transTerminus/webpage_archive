# 金星：变性手术使我面临终身残废 被至亲陷害遭拘留

![金星变性手术](https://dolphin.deliver.ifeng.com/c?z=ifeng&la=0&si=2&ci=23&cg=22&c=29&or=232&l=715&bg=715&b=713&u=https://y0.ifengimg.com/34c4a1d78882290c/2012/0528/1x1.gif)
