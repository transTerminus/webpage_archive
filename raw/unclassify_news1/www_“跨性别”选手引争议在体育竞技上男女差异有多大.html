Phishing alert


You were redirected to this page because your computer attempted to visit a known unsafe website or a known phishing link.

 

Typically, this is a result from clicking on a harmful link or attachment, even if it appears legitimate. 

To protect yourself, never click on unknown links or open unfamiliar attachements you receive in unsolicited emails, popups, texts, chats, or social networking sites.  If you don't know who the message is from DELETE IT.

If you receive spam or phising messages in your UCSC Google email account, report the email to Google, then delete it. 

If you believe you were redirected to this page from a legitimate source, please report the incident. 

To learn how to protect your information and privacy, follow our guidance on How to Stay Secure.
